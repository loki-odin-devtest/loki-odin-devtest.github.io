VOCAL MUSIC LESSONS . . .

A LIFETIME OF JOY

Congratulations on choosing to participate in music lessons. Music has been found to enrich the lives of all who participate in its creations or in its enjoyment. When you choose to take vocal music lessons you are choosing to learn a skill that will benefit you and bring you joy your entire life! No price can be put on such a beautiful art as music, in any form. Yet, obtaining the skill will certainly require assistance from a professional—your music teacher . . . Me.

I have chosen to make music my occupation. As with any business, costs are calculated to both ensure that the highest quality product is given and that my business makes enough to continue providing my services and expertise.

What does my yearly tuition cover?

All independent music teachers (IMTs) differ in methods of operation; however your tuition covers the following:

  • Time spent with the student

Lessons, performances

  • Time spent in preparation for the student

Trips to the store, lesson planning, bookkeeping, development of curriculum, etc. It has been cited by professional music journals that to every hour enrolled in music lessons, you are investing in up to 2 hours of the teacher’s time

  • My training and experience

Including my accompaniment at lessons, recitals and festivals.

  • Recital costs and preparations

Programs, facility rental, equipment

  • Studio expenses

Copying, computer software, incentive programs, office supplies, auto expenses, etc. . . .

  • Music books and recordings

Materials purchased by the teacher to keep costs low for students

  • Professional organization memberships

Memberships are maintained to enhance teaching skills and provide the student with opportunities for festivals, competitions, and performance events.

  • Professional journals

Publications to assist in keeping current on new materials and trends.

  • Self-employment taxes, insurance, retirement

The IMT has no corporation to assist in providing health and life insurance. Tuition helps over that increased living cost of self-employment.
